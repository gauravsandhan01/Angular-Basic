Thompson Seedless

Light green, sweet; perfect for eating, raisins, and wine.

Black Corinth (Zante Currant)

Small black grapes; used mainly for dried currants, intensely sweet.

Flame Seedless

Bright red; crisp and mildly sweet, great for fresh eating.

Concord

Dark purple; iconic "grape" taste, great for juice and jelly.

Red Globe

Large red grapes; juicy and mildly sweet, great as table grapes.

Crimson Seedless

Crimson red; firm, sweet table grape with good shelf life.

Moon Drops

Unique long black grapes; extra sweet and crunchy.

Autumn Royal

Large black/purple grapes; sweet flavor and seedless.

Cotton Candy

Green grapes with a fun cotton candy-like flavor.

Kyoho

Large dark purple grapes; very juicy and sweet, popular in Asia.
